Volatility — ATR & ADR

Wilder's ATR accounts for gaps (max of today's High-Low, |High-prevClose|, |Low-prevClose|), so it moves more than a plain range average on gap days — which matters here given how often NIFTY gaps. ADR is the plain average, shown at a few lookback lengths so you can see whether volatility is expanding or settling.
